# CNNA finite-core manuscript — v12 changelog

**Version date:** 2026-07-17  
**Previous frozen snapshot:** 2026-07-16 (v11)  
**Change class:** literature review, bibliographic metadata, and contextualization only

## Added

- A new section, **“Recent related work and model separation”**, placing the finite CNNA construction against current work on:
  - growing binary and \(b\)-ary trees;
  - increasing, sequential, synchronous, and stochastic tree-growth models;
  - Dirichlet-to-Neumann maps and inverse problems on weighted tree graphs;
  - Schur reduction and effective response operators on finite networks;
  - directed graph Laplacians and related elimination procedures.
- An explicit comparison matrix distinguishing:
  - primitive carrier;
  - what grows (carrier, subtree, occupation, or state);
  - deterministic versus stochastic event order;
  - sequential versus synchronous growth;
  - preservation versus rearrangement of prior structure;
  - immutable birth provenance versus later-updated live response;
  - forward construction versus inverse recovery of response data.
- Recent references from 2022–2026 where directly relevant.
- DOI metadata and persistent links for references wherever verifiably available.

## Clarified

- The comparison with existing literature does **not** claim an exhaustive novelty theorem.
- Classical primary sources remain in place; recent work is added to connect the foundational literature to the current research context.
- The paper’s finite claim boundary remains unchanged.
